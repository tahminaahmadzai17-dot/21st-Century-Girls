About the Program

The 21st Century Girls Program teaches girls who have been denied access to education the skills needed for the modern world. The program focuses on engineering, robotics, coding, innovation, teamwork, and leadership.

The program uses project-based learning and teamwork to build problem solving skills and prepare students for future STEM careers.

Mission

Our mission is to empower girls with the knowledge and technological skills needed to participate in the global workforce and continue their education in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics.

Program Courses

1. 21st Century Technologies and Skills

Students learn robotics, engineering, and coding. They create digital games, learn block coding, and explore STEM careers.

2. Advanced 21st Century Skills – Python Coding

Students develop advanced coding skills using Python, including functions, loops, decision making, and problem solving.

3. 21st Century Emerging Talent

Students work in teams to create projects in leadership, AI, analytics, and innovation that solve real world problems.

4. Teaching 21st Century Skills

Training for teaching assistants and instructors to develop leadership and teaching abilities using project based learning.
